Brahmakalasha

This song plays during the early ritual scenes and sets the mood of the movie. The slow beats and chants show the deep link between the people and their god. It feels serious and old, showing that these beliefs have existed for generations. The song makes the land feel sacred, not just owned. It helps the audience understand that faith controls life in the village.

Rebel Song

This song appears when the story focuses on ancestors and duty. The strong rhythm shows strength, unity, and responsibility. It explains how protecting the land is not a choice but a duty passed down. The voices sound firm, showing pride and sacrifice. This song connects past warriors to the present struggle.

Madana Mana Mohini

This song plays when the kingdom or outsiders try to take control. The tone feels tense and heavy, showing danger and imbalance. It contrasts with the ritual songs, showing clash between belief and power. The music builds pressure, hinting that something bad will happen. This song highlights why the conflict is serious and unavoidable.
